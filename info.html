This is a simple tool to calculate the chance of rebounding after stopping phototherapy in neonates.

See calculator

This calculator is based on the formula published in A Clinical Prediction Rule for Rebound Hyperbilirubinemia Following Inpatient Phototherapy, Pearl W. Chang, et al. Pediatrics Feb 2017

The formula calculates a score utilising the current SBR and it's relation to the AAP 2004 guidelines on phototherapy.

The code for the calculator is available freely here.
